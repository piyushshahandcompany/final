Professional Services

Piyush Shah and Company provides a comprehensive range of accounting, taxation, audit and regulatory compliance services. Our services are designed to support businesses and individuals in meeting statutory obligations, managing financial risks, and making informed decisions in a complex regulatory environment.

With over 25 years of professional experience and a PAN India service reach, the firm delivers structured, reliable and practical solutions across multiple sectors and business structures.

Accounting and Bookkeeping Services

Accurate accounting forms the foundation of effective financial management and statutory compliance. We provide end-to-end accounting and bookkeeping services tailored to the size, nature and complexity of each client’s operations.

Our services include maintenance of books of accounts, preparation of periodic financial statements, reconciliation of accounts, and review of accounting records to ensure compliance with applicable accounting standards and statutory requirements. We work closely with management to ensure that accounting data reflects the true financial position of the business.

By adopting a structured and disciplined approach, we help clients maintain accurate records, improve financial transparency, and facilitate timely decision-making. Our accounting services are delivered with a strong focus on confidentiality, consistency and regulatory compliance.

Direct and Indirect Taxation

Taxation is a critical area requiring continuous monitoring of legal developments and careful application of tax laws. We provide advisory and compliance services under direct and indirect tax laws to individuals, firms, companies and other entities.

Our services include income tax compliance, tax return filings, tax planning, representation before tax authorities, and advisory on complex tax matters. In the area of indirect taxation, we assist clients with Goods and Services Tax (GST) registrations, return filings, reconciliations, assessments and advisory.

Our approach focuses on lawful tax optimisation, risk mitigation and timely compliance. We ensure that clients remain aligned with evolving tax regulations while maintaining clarity and transparency in their tax positions.

Audit and Assurance

Audit and assurance services play a vital role in enhancing the credibility of financial information. We conduct statutory audits, tax audits, internal audits and other assurance engagements in accordance with applicable laws and professional standards.

Our audit approach is risk-based and process-oriented, focusing on understanding the client’s business environment, internal controls and compliance framework. We aim to provide independent and objective insights that add value beyond statutory requirements.

Through our audit and assurance services, we assist clients in strengthening governance, improving internal systems and ensuring compliance with regulatory expectations.

Regulatory and Compliance Services

Businesses today operate in an environment of increasing regulatory oversight. We provide comprehensive compliance services to ensure adherence to corporate, tax and other statutory requirements.

Our services include company law compliance, maintenance of statutory records, filing of regulatory returns, and advisory on compliance frameworks. We work proactively to help clients meet deadlines and avoid regulatory exposure.

By offering structured compliance support, we enable clients to focus on their core business activities with confidence and peace of mind.
