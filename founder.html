CA Piyush Shah

CA Piyush Shah is the Founder and Proprietor of Piyush Shah and Company, a Mumbai-based Chartered Accountant firm with over 25 years of professional experience. His practice is built on strong technical foundations, professional discipline, and a long-term advisory approach.

Over the years, he has advised a wide range of clients including individuals, family-owned businesses, partnerships, companies and institutions across multiple sectors. His work spans accounting, taxation, audit, regulatory compliance and advisory services.
